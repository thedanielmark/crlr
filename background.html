BACKGROUND

The ‘Chittoor Rural Leprosy Rehabilitation & Social Welfare Association’ - (CRLR&SWA) is a ‘Humanitarian, Health-care Service Provider & Rehabilitation Centre’ with the mission to ‘detect, treat, cure or sustain and rehabilitate the people affected with Leprosy, HIV/AIDS’ and TB’, providing them a renewed social identity, hope and means to survive with fullness! The CRLR&SWA was initiated in response to the inhuman plight of thousands of people affected with Leprosy who were stigmatized including their children and the way the society forced them to live in seclusion amidst life threatening situations barring them from public life, common water resources, labour market and socio-cultural activities…just to name a few. Lack of education, awareness and livelihood support system coupled with fear and religious misgivings… are some of the most fundamental reasons why there’s a prevalence of high percentage of these diseases in the district. Thus, ‘CRLR&SWA’ was born to reach out to the helpless and disabled people affected by these diseases through community mobilization, mass awareness education and empowerment at various levels by active Search, Education & Treatment (SET) in remote villages and farming communities, who otherwise wouldn’t get socio-economic and educational justices to end their inhuman sufferings & isolation.

The ‘CRLR&SWA’ has got recognitions from all quarters of the communities and the State as a Centre for Credible Rehabilitation & a Health-care Service Provider. Till date ‘CRLR&SWA’ has cured and rehabilitated more than 20,000 leprosy patients in Chittoor District of Andhra Pradesh. The ‘CRLR&SWA’ was registered in the State of Andhra Pradesh in 1991 as Charitable Society and subsequently with the Govt. of India with all legal formalities.
